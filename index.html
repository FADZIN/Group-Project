About Us

Pusat Tuisyen Utama Baru is a dedicated learning center committed to helping students achieve academic excellence. We provide a positive, interactive, and focused learning environment, suitable for students from primary to secondary levels. Since our establishment, we have guided many students to improve their performance in core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, Malay Language, and English. Our team of educators consists of experienced and passionate teachers who understand each student's individual learning needs.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality supplementary education through effective and student-friendly approaches.

Our Vision

To become the preferred tuition center that nurtures knowledgeable, disciplined, and high-achieving students.

Our Core Values

  • Commitment to quality education.
  • Responsibility and integrity.
  • Lifelong learning.
  • Academic excellence.
